About Me

My name is Hamza Naeem. I am a 30 year old born in Lahore, Pakistan. I was born in the year 1987 and moved to the United States as an immagrant with my parents who were seeking room for advancement. I moved around alot in my childhood to cities like Queens, Houston, St. Louis, San Antonio, and Portland respectively. I graduated high school from Tom C. Clark High School in San Antonio. I then attended University of Texas at San Antonio and then was offered a football scholarship to Culver-Stockton College where I played collegiate football for two years before moving back to San Antonio to finish up at University of Texas at San Antonio(UTSA) where I graduated with a Bachelors degree in Biology with a Minor in Chemisrty. I began working as a non tenured lecturer at UTSA then moved to Portland to begin my life as a programmer in 2017. My hobbies include; playing basketball, going on hikes, reading, and now coding. The reason I chose the life of a programmer is because of the high demand for the trade and my uncle who has been in the tech industry suggested I check out Epicodus specifiacally and that his company would hire graduates from Epicodus on the spot. In combination with the lack of room for advancement and improvment career-wise in my previous field of work and the high demand in this new industry I have now chosen I feel strongly about my desicion to move forward in my new field.
